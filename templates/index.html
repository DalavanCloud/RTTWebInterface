Randomness Testing Toolkit

This service allows users to easily and quickly test randomness of their binary data. The data are analysed with following statistical software.

  • NIST Statistical Test Suite
  • Dieharder
  • TestU01

RTT was developed at Centre for Research on Cryptography and Security, Faculty of Informatics, Masaryk University.

Project GitHub CRoCS FI MUNI

Test your data

You can use our tool and servers to analyse your data. You don't have to install anything, you only need to provide data for analysis. The service is available only for authorized users, please write us to lubomir.obratil@gmail.com and we will create an account for you.

We do not store analysed data.
